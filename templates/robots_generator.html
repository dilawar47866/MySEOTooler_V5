 Common Robots.txt Patterns

                


                    

                        Block Admin Areas:
                        
/admin/
 /wp-admin/
 /login/

                    


                    

                        Block Search/Filter Pages:
                        
/*?s=
 /*?filter=
 /search/

                    


                    

                        Block Staging/Test Sites:
                        
/staging/
 /test/
 /dev/

                    


                    

                        Block Resource Files:
                        
/assets/
 /css/
 /js/

                    


                

            


            
            

                
 Best Practices

                
    
                    
  • Always include sitemap: Helps search engines find your content
    • 
                    
  • Don't block CSS/JS: Google needs these to render pages
    • 
                    
  • Use with caution: Wrong config can harm your SEO
    • 
                    
  • Test first: Use Google Search Console robots.txt tester
    • 
                    
  • Case sensitive: /Admin/ and /admin/ are different
